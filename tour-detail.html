An Afternoon Stroll At Vaxholm

Discover the picturesque town of Vaxholm, Sweden - the crown jewel of the Stockholm Archipelago. Our self guided virtual tour with videos show every corner reveals Vaxholm's enduring charm: from the iconic Vaxholm Fortress to the history of women’s life in a fishing village. Feast your eyes on the cheerful array of wooden houses, each painted with the most vivid colors, embodying Sweden's effortless blend of tradition and modernity. Don't forget to treat yourself to an authentic Fika experience in a nonprofit cozy local café. An afternoon stroll in Vaxholm isn't merely a walk, it's a sensory journey that captivates and enchants.

Information provided by: Sveriges Hembyhdsforbund

Svergies Hembygdsforbund, or the Swedish Local Heritage Federation, is a nationwide organization that works to preserve and promote Swedish cultural heritage. This dynamic federation encompasses over 2,000 local heritage associations, connecting individuals who are passionate about Sweden's history, cultural traditions, and local environments. Through initiatives such as local events, educational programs, and advocacy efforts, Sveriges Hembygdsförbund seeks to ensure that the richness of Swedish heritage continues to thrive for future generations.
